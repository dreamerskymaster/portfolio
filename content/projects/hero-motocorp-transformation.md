# Hero MotoCorp Manufacturing Transformation

## Overview
At Hero MotoCorp, India's largest two-wheeler manufacturer, I led a comprehensive plant optimization initiative. The project focused on modernizing legacy infrastructure and implementing Industry 4.0 technologies to improve efficiency and reliability.

## Key Achievements
*   **Downtime Reduction**: Achieved an 83% reduction in equipment downtime through predictive maintenance and TPM implementation.
*   **Cost Savings**: Generated significant annual savings through process optimization and tool cost reduction.
*   **EV Production**: Increased Vida EV production capacity by 23% through fixture design optimization.

## Methodologies
*   **TPM (Total Productive Maintenance)**: Implemented Autonomous and Planned Maintenance pillars.
*   **Six Sigma**: Utilized DMAIC methodology for process improvement.
*   **Digital Transformation**: Integrated IoT sensors and SCADA systems for real-time monitoring.
