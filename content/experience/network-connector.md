# Network Connector

As a Network Connector, I played a pivotal role in supporting the student community at Northeastern University. This role involved mentoring students, organizing events, and providing critical logistical support.

## Key Responsibilities

*   **Community Building**: Fostered a strong network among students specializing in manufacturing and engineering.
*   **Event Management**: Planned and executed monthly events to facilitate networking and knowledge sharing.
*   **Student Support**: Assisted students with finding accommodation, understanding commute options, and settling into the city.
*   **Mentorship**: Provided guidance on academic and career paths within the manufacturing sector.
